Frequently Asked Questions

Constructo is a comprehensive construction cost estimation platform designed specifically for individuals building homes on small plots (under 1500 sq ft) with budgets between ₹1 Cr and ₹1.5 Cr.

Our platform helps you plan your construction project with detailed cost breakdowns, sample house plans, product selection guides, and expert tips to avoid common construction mistakes.

Our cost estimates are based on current market rates for materials and labor in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities. We regularly update our database to ensure accuracy.

However, actual costs may vary based on your specific location, contractor negotiations, and market fluctuations. We recommend using our estimates as a starting point for your budget planning.

Free users get 3 basic estimates and access to limited sample plans.

Premium subscribers enjoy unlimited estimates, detailed cost breakdowns with specific products and brands, labor cost calculations, expert tips, and access to our complete library of sample plans.

Yes, premium users can customize their estimates by selecting specific materials, brands, and quality tiers (Eco+, Standard, or Premium).

This allows you to see how different choices impact your overall budget and make informed decisions about where to invest more and where to save.

Our Sample Plan Selector allows you to enter your plot dimensions (width and length), orientation, and desired number of floors.
